Welcome to "The Monkees" official Website!

Here you will find all things Monkees. Want to delve into our back catalogue? Have a look at our discography section, with interactive slider. Our media section contains both video and audio files for your viewing as well as links to other sources, such as spotify where you can hear more from us.

Want to see us live? The tour section of the site will list upcoming tour dates. Can't make it to our show? Why not book us for your next event? Yes, you read correctly! The monkees are now available for private functions. If you are interested, simply head to the bookings page and fill out the form. A member of our booking team should respond to your inquiry within 48 hours of submitting.

Finally, If you want to buy our albums or merchandise, simply follow the Shop link provided. Also see our Facebook, Twitter and Youtube links. Never miss out on the latest Monkees news and releases ever again!
