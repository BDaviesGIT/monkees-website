Daydream Believer

Here we have the muisic video for "Daydream believer", arguably one of the band's mantle singles. Composed by John Steward, the single found world wide chart success, hitting the number one spot in the US and Ireland and reached number 5 in the Uk Singles chart.

In discussing the themes of the song, John Steward noted the track was about the developing relationship of married couples in that the first few years of marrage are a idealistic haze, but this gradually wears off and it is at this point that true love is found.
